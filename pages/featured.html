Housing Inspection Workshop on April 18th!

The availability of quality, affordable housing continues to be one of the largest challenges faced by communities in the MIDAS region. Many of the region’s communities are not able to afford to hire a building inspector for rental inspections or construction inspections. MIDAS is planning an effort to determine if there is enough support to establish a MIDAS housing inspection program.

On April 18th, every City and County in the MIDAS region is invited to a workshop to discuss the need for a housing inspection service. The meeting will be from 1:00 – 3:00 at the Fort Dodge Public Library. City Clerks, Mayors, Council Persons, or any representative designated may attend on behalf of the City or County. If no one can attend from your community, written input will be accepted.

Information we are looking to collect and plan to discuss at the meeting includes:

  • Current inspection programs, if any.
  • Current inspections perfromed by your staff or contractor.
  • Interest in participating in a MIDAS inspections program.
  • Estimated number of annual rental inspections needed.
  • Estimated number of annual construction inspections needed.
  • Housing codes now being used.
