DART - Dodger Area Rapid Transit System


For local Dart Bus service, call: (515)-573-8145


For other Transit Service, call: 1-800-BUS-RIDE


DART Fares:

Adults: $1.50

Seniors and Students: $1.25

Montly passes sold too!


Days/Hours of Operation

Monday - Friday (except holidays)

7:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m.


Peak Hour Service

All buses run every half hour during these time periods:

7:00 a.m. - 8:30 a.m.

2:30 p.m. - 4:30 p.m.


Regular Hour Service

8:30 a.m. - 2:30 p.m. - call for schedule

4:30 p.m. - 6:00 p.m. - call for schedule


Para Transit Service

7:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m. - Monday through Friday

Call the day before your trip or earlier to schedule your ride.

Same day rides will have an additional charge of $1.00.
